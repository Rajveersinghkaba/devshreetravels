Explore Outdoor attractions of Rajasthan

About

Rajasthan, the "Land of Kings," is known for its stunning outdoor attractions that showcase its rich history, culture, and natural beauty. Here are some of the best outdoor attractions in Rajasthan:

Highlights:

    1.Thar Desert (Great Indian Desert)

  • Enjoy camel safaris in Jaisalmer and the Sam Sand Dunes.
  • Experience traditional Rajasthani music and dance during desert camps.
  • Witness breathtaking sunsets over the dunes.

    • 2. Udaipur Lakes

  • Lake Pichola: Take a boat ride and visit Jag Mandir and Lake Palace
  • Fateh Sagar Lake: Relax by the lake or enjoy water sports.

    • 3. Mount Abu

  • Rajasthan's only hill station, known for its scenic beauty.
  • Visit Nakki Lake, Dilwara Temples, and Guru Shikhar (highest peak of the Aravallis).

    • 3.Ranthambore National Park

  • Famous for its tiger population and diverse wildlife
  • Visit Ranthambore Fort, located within the park.
